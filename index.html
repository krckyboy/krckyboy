About #1

Hi! I'm John Smith from Houston, Texas and I'm 23 years old. My work, love and passion is photography. What I like to do the most is travel and make amazing photographs.

About #2

I put my energy into providing the best possible photographs to my clients in an array of formats. I'm available 24/7 and for every gig that I accept, I take very seriously and push myself to the limit.

My Services

PHOTO SHOOTING

Photo shooting either on-site or in-studio. I do portrait, corporate, event, architectural, and of course landscape photography.

EDITING

Editing photographs in a high-end software that either a photographer of your choice or myself made.

PRINTING

Printing individuals photographs or series in the form of a book or album of photos that you select.
